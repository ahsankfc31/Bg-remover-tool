How to Remove Background from Image

Our background remover tool makes it easy to create transparent backgrounds for your images. Follow these simple steps:

  1. Upload your image by clicking the "Select Image" button or dragging and dropping your file
  2. Adjust the compression level if needed (higher quality for detailed edges)
  3. Select your preferred output format (PNG for transparency)
  4. Wait a few seconds while our AI processes your image
  5. Download your image with transparent background
